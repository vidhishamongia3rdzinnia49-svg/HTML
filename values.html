UPCOMING MUN

Galgotias University (GU) organizes and provides training for Model United Nations (MUN) to its students, fostering skills in public speaking, leadership, and diplomacy. The university has hosted events like the Galgotias International MUN (GIMUN) and offers training programs through its School of Liberal Education (SOLE), with collaboration from external agencies. Students can get involved through official clubs and societies, training sessions, and by participating in the conferences themselves

Techfest World Model United Nations (TWMUN) 2025 is an upcoming event at the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay (IIT Bombay) on December 23-24, 2025. It is a Model UN conference for school and college students to engage in diplomatic simulations, with committees like the Economic and Financial Affairs Council (ECOFIN) and the Disarmament and International Security Committee (DISEC). The event is part of the larger Techfest festival, which is Asia's largest science and technology festival.

Upcoming Model United Nations (MUN) conferences include Joust MUN'25 in Noida (Nov 8-9, 2025), Savanna MUN in Delhi (Dec 6-7, 2025), and AMIMUN 2026 in Noida (Jan 16-18, 2026). Galgotias University MUN 2025 is in Greater Noida (Nov 29-30, 2025), and online options like DMUN Arab League 2025 (Nov 15) are also available.
