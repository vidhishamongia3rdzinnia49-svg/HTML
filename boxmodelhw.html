Key initiatives
Viksit Bharat@2047: A call for citizen ideas to shape the nation's future. Citizens can submit their ideas online. Viksit Bharat Youth Parliament: A platform for young people (ages 18-25) to deliberate on national issues and contribute to the vision for 2047. Viksit Bharat Buildathon: A student innovation movement for classes 6-12 to develop solutions for real-world problems under themes like Atmanirbhar Bharat (self-reliant India) and Vocal for Local. Viksit Bharat Rozgar Yojana: A program to boost employment, improve skills, and enhance social security, with a special focus on the manufacturing sector. Viksit Bharat Run: A global initiative to mobilize citizens and diaspora in a collective effort for nation-building.
